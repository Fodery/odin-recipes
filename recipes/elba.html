Elba Kuwaiti Sweet

Elba is a traditional dessert from Kuwait ..

they used to make with Colostrums (also known as first milk ) of cows or goats because of its benefits and the amount of protein it has ( poor baby ) but nowadays we’re using regular milk to make it , I prefer the dried kind and it should be full fat milk otherwise creme fresh or thick cream

Ingredients

  • 6 large eggs
  • 1 cup condensed milk
  • 2 cups of powdered ( dried ) milk
  • 5 cups of water
  • Ground cardamom & saffron to taste

Recipe Instructions

  1. preheat the oven to 200-210 c
  2. mix all ingredient
  3. Pour it in ramekin or pyrex or any container with a lid
  4. place it in water bath ” bain marie” in the oven for hour and half
  5. Let it cool completely in the fridge before serving ( preferably overnight)
