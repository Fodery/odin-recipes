My Secret Recipe For A Luxury Tea

This Tea can transform you into great Philosopher

are ready?

Ingredients (1 cup)

  • Small spoon fine black tea
  • only 1 whole cardamom
  • Little of Saffron
  • Few drops of Rose Water
  • Sweetener of your choice

Recipe Instructions

  1. Boil the water then let it calm
  2. Add all the ingredient in a glass cup
  3. Mix them with the boiled water
  4. Wait 5 min
  5. Enjoy
